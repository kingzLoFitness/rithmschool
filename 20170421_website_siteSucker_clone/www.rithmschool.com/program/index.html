{ Small Classes + Experienced Educators  }

High Quality Instruction

Our dedicated instructors have years of experience both teaching and writing curriculum. Our classes are small, so you can move faster and cover more material in less time, and our curriculum is industry-relevant, enabling you to hit the ground running.

Become a Professional Web Developer

Contribute to real-world projects or open source, gain a deep understanding of the industry's conventions and best practices, and master the technical skills you need to succeed in the workforce.

Our Commitment

Our program is fast-paced and requires a lot from our students. If we see you struggling, we do everything we can to help. If you are not seeing success after the first 6 weeks, your time as a student will come to an end and we will offer you a full refund, minus your enrollment deposit. We will still support you in enrolling in a future class if you wish.

Exceptional Peers + Community

We’re committed to creating a phenomenal learning environment. Not only does that mean high quality instructors and an amazing campus, it also means admitting students who are fully committed to learning to code and who will fully immerse themselves in the Rithm experience. You’ll spend so much time working with and learning from your peers, so it’s vital that everyone is invested in the program and ready to take an active role in their learning.

Tuition Refund + Guarantee

Our intention is to foster every student’s growth, and that means we’ll never push you to graduate or finish the program if it’s not in your best interest. We also provide a full refund (minus your enrollment deposit) if you do not find a job in web development six months after graduating.

Your Commitment

We are seeking students who are ready to embrace the immersion experience head-on, are lovers of problem solving and committed to learning code. Enthusiastic, collaborative learners thrive at Rithm.

Prework

Plan to spend 80-120 hours mastering fundamentals. We'll provide 4 hours of one-on-one support to make sure you're set up for success.

Our prework consists of the following free courses:

  • Terminal and UNIX
  • Git and Github
  • HTML and CSS
  • Intermediate CSS and Bootstrap
  • Intermediate JavaScript Part I
  • Intermediate JavaScript Part II

Full-time coursework

Full-time classes last 13 weeks, and are based at Rithm's San Francisco campus in the Mission. Classes are held Monday - Friday, 9 am - 6 pm.

What you'll be doing on any given day may vary, but you'll typically be coding for a majority of the day, either alone or in pairs. Instructors will lecture on more conceptual topics in the morning and afternoon, but we encourage you to spend as much time as possible coding while instructors are available. In the later afternoon and evenings, you'll have material to read, watch, and review in order to solidify your understanding and prepare for the next day.

Part-time interview prep

Two weeks before the end of the full-time program, Rithm students are encouraged to apply to Outco. Upon admission, part-time instruction will commence and students will begin the job hunt. This portion of the program is based at Outco's campus in downtown San Francisco. Classes are held Monday - Thursday 6 pm - 9 pm.

Tuition Details

The cost of our program depends on whether or not you plan on attending Outco. Attendance to Outco is strongly encouraged, but is not required.

Rithm School:  $19,800

This includes:

  • 4 hours of 1:1 instruction before your course start date. Meet the team.
  • 13 weeks of full-time classroom instruction. Learn more about the curriculum.
  • A network of over 100 portfolio companies for hiring assistance and continued job support. Learn more about job support.

Rithm School + Outco:  $24,000

This includes:

  • Everything included in the base Rithm tuition
  • 5 weeks of part-time interview prep with Outco
  • A full tuition refund (minus your enrollment deposit) if you do not find a job as a developer after six months of completing our program. See Details.

Payment

A $3,000 deposit is due upon enrollment. For the remaining balance, you have two options:

  • Pay the remainder on the first day of class,
  • Pay one installment of half of the remainder on the first day of class, and the other half on the first day of week seven.

Please note that paying in installments involves an additional fee of $500.
